Login window

TravelMart Website ››

1. Log-in window display:

1.1 To log-in, type your User Name.

1.2 Type your Password.

1.3 Click the Log In button.

       Note: For new users, it will ask you to change your password if you haven't changed it yet.


1.4 To change password, type your current password.

1.5 Type your new password.

1.6 Re-type your new password to confirm.

1.7 Click Save button to Save your new password.

1.8 Otherwise, Click Cancel button to ignore change password.
